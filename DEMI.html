Outer Aperture

The outer section of DEMI is two hinging doors. The first is a quarter circle. In our protoype, this door has a polycarbonate cover. In practice this would be aluminum as with all other parts. The second door is a flat flap that can move within the circular door while minimizing gaps.

Upon approaching a piece of junk, both doors open. Then once the object is in grabbing range, the doors close, encapsulating the object.

Inner Aperture

The inner section of DEMI takes inspriation from a garage door. Two motors, one on each side work to raise and lower the two sides of the door. The motors are attached to the doors with L chanels as seen on the front.

When an object becomes contained by the outer doors, the inner doors roll open. Then the outer flat door rotates within the circular door and pushes the object through and into the final storage area.

The inners then roll closed again, securing the object so that DEMI can capture the next piece of junk.
