Arduino Solar Tracker

Problem: Design a solar tracker that can automatically turn a solar panel perpendicular to the sun to maximize its efficiency.
Solussion: we designed a 2 DOF manipulator that could be controlled from an Arduino. It used 2 photo resistors with a shadow casting divider between them to determine the sun's orientation. If one sensor saw more light than the other, the manipulator would turn in that direction. If both saw equal light, the dollar panel would be staring right at the sun.
